* Query Solving using Mongo Shell *

Q1.Query the collection ""employee"" and list all the documents

→ Command to query the collection of employee:
    db.employee.find().pretty()

Q2.Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are having salary more than 30000 

→ Command to query the collection of employee  who are having salary more than 30000:
    db.employee.find({"salary":{"$gt":"30000"}}).pretty()

Q3.Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are having experience more than 2 years. 

→ Command to query the collection of employee  who are having experience more than 2 years:
    db.employee.find({"overallExp":{"$gt":"2"}}).pretty()

Q4.Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are graduated after 2015 and having experience more than 1 year 

→ Command to query the collection of employee who are graduated after 2015 and having experience more than 1 year:
    db.employee.find({"yearGrad":{"$gt":"2015"}},
            {"overallExp":{"$gt":"2"}}).pretty()

Q5.Query the collection ""employee"" and update the salary of the employee whose salary is greater than 70000 to 65000. 

→ Command to query the collection of employee  and update the salary of the employee whose salary is greater than 70000 to 65000. :
    db.employee.updateMany({"salary":{"$gt":"70000"}},{"$set":{"salary":"90000"}}).pretty()

Q6.Delete all the documents from ""employee"" where last company is Y". 

→ Command to Delete all the documents from ""employee"" where last company is Y"
    db.employee.deleteMany({"lastcompany":"Y" }).pretty()
